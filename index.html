About the product

Bliss is a web and mobile application that allows you to schedule and customize your beauty and personal care services in one place. Designed to enhance the quality of service and management of centers offering beauty and personal care services.

Features

Customize your beauty service to your liking
Enhance with the reviews of your clients.
Get schedule visualization to improve your sessions.
Access to an easy-to-use payment system.
Control your personalization limits and staff.

Our Mission

Our Vision

"Enhancing the quality of service and management of centers offering beauty and personal care services."
